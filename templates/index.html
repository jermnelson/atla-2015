BIBFRAME - short for Bibliographic Framework - is a new (for libraries) linked-data vocabulary and specifications initiated and sponsored by the Library of Congress as a replacement for the MARC21 format.

Learning Outcomes:

  1. Learn about the general concepts behind Linked Data and Libraries
  2. Overview of major BIBFRAME classes and concepts
  3. Understand how MARC is mapped being mapped to BIBFRAME
  4. Exposure to emerging technologies based on or using BIBFRAME
