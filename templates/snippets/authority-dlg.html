BIBFRAME Authorities

BIBFRAME authorities are defined resources representing a key concept or thing that have a domain, an entity responsible for the management, promotion, and organization of the authority.

{#

Works and Instances link with these different concepts and things that capture different relationships like creators, subjects, and publishers.

#}

  • Agent

    The Agent class is an entity that has a role (Person, Organization, etc.) in a resource.

    Person

    The Person Authority in BIBFRAME is an individual or an identity established by an individual(such as a pseudonym)

    Organization

    An Organization Authority in BIBFRAME is a corporation, group of organization, or general grouping of people acting as a unit.

  • Place

    A Place authority is a geographic location either real or imagined.

  • Temporal Concept

    A Temporal Concept authority is a either real or imagined Chronological period.

  • Topic

    A Topic authority is a concept or area of knowledge
