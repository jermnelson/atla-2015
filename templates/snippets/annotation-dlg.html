BIBFRAME Annotations

A BIBFRAME Annotation is a resource that asserts additional information about other BIBFRAME Works, Instances, Authorities, or even other Annotations. Specific Annotation types include:

  • Cover Art

    The Cover Art Annotation is a link to an illustration of a BIBFRAME Instance

  • Held Material

    The Held Material Annotation contains summary holdings information for an Organization's BIBFRAME Instances. The HeldMaterial subclass, HeldItem, contains specific holding information for an Item.

  • Review

    The Review Annotation is a critique of a resource, such as a Movie review or a book comment on Amazon.

  • Table of Contents

    The TableOfContents Annotation is a simple the table of contents information for a resource.
